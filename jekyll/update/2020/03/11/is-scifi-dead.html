Is Sci-fi Running Out of Futuristic Ideas?

Mar 11, 2020

A wall peppered with a lot of screens and touch screens were consifred futuristics and fit for a sci-fi space ship. In blade runner we had futuristics building and in star wars, interior designs. But interior design has progress to a point where we can and do see many of these futuristic design in some porche office or malls. It is to a point where we can’t design a futuristic looking interior and more. Look at Star Trek Picard’s Federation head quarters. Doesn’t look any different from an office we would see today. In many ways, we have all those things now. We have phones, GPS maps, etc. So what sci-fi do we look forward to now?
